Privacy Policy for Marmaris App

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Marmaris App is a city and travel guide for Marmaris. The app is designed to work without account registration.

Data Collection

Marmaris App does not collect personal information, does not require login, and does not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Favorites and local notes are stored on the user's device.

Network Services

The app may load travel images, weather information, map-related data, duty pharmacy pages, venue websites, and other public web links when the user opens related app sections or external links.

These external services may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser or app request metadata, and device network information according to their own privacy practices.

Location

Marmaris App does not require precise location access for its core guide content. If the user opens maps or external directions, map providers may process location or route information according to their own privacy policies.

Contact
