Marmaris App

A calm iOS travel guide for Marmaris, Icmeler, Turunc, Selimiye, Bozburun and nearby bays.

Open Support Issue Privacy Policy

Plan Coastal Days

Browse beaches, marinas, viewpoints, restaurants, hotels, family activities and local services around Marmaris.

Use Local Tools

Open maps, save favorites, keep local notes and check curated service links such as duty pharmacies.

No Login Required

Favorites and notes stay on your device. The app does not collect personal information.
